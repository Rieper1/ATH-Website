Key Characters in Hitman Lore


Agent 47

Agent 47 is the protagonist and playable character within the Hitman universe. An orphaned clone, 47 was raised and conditioned to kill in a brutal and nurtureless Romanian asylum under Dr Ort-Meyer. Eventually escaping the clutches of his merciless captors, 47 began a career of covert assassination and espionage for the nuetral International Contract Agency.

Details

Diana Burnwood

Diana Burnwood is the dueteragonist within the Hitman universe. Diana succumbed to a dedicated career of handling highly dangerous and lethal assassins for ICA, after she tragically lost her parents and younger brother to corrupt pharmaceutical Blue Seed executives. She came to handle Agent 47 exclusively. After twenty years, the two had developed an infallible bond.

Details

Lucas Grey

Lucas Grey is the tritagonist within the Hitman universe. Grey was raised in the same hostile environment as 47's clone 'brother' under the codename Agent 6. He escaped sometime prior to 47 with his memory fully intact. Working in private security under his new alias, Grey eventually discovered Providence, an elite cabal behind their barbaric upbringing. He eventually recruited 47 to wage a joined vendetta of annihilation against the global criminal conglomerate.

Details

The Constant

The Constant, otherwise known as Arthur Edwards, is the main antagonist within the Hitman universe. Edwards is the chief of Providence. His coveted position comes with the esteemed responsiblity of ensuring that his cabal empire remains ever powerful and continues to expand. There are hardly any facts surrounding his true origins. His leadership is closely monitored by a mysterious board known only as The Partners; who seem to wield the true power.

Details

Agent Smith

Carlton Smith is a recurring comedic character within the Hitman universe. Agent Smith is a blundering and clumsy operative who has worked for notable agencies like the CIA and Interpol. 47 has encountered him several times throughout his career - frequently having to pull him out of avoidable tight spots. Smith has often repaid 47 in kind by providing him with invaluable mission intel.
