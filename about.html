About


All Things Hitman is a gaming Youtube Channel that is devoted to the Hitman series. What makes it stand out from other Hitman channels is that it focuses strongly on Hitman Lore. That is, the mysteriously intriguing and complex characters and universe behind the franchise. This channel hosts numerous analytical video essays that delve deeply into the minds, motivations and aspirations of many of the Hitman characters and their unique associations with one another. These videos offer viewers incredibly rare insight into the world of Agent 47.

Additionally, this channel offers a diverse range of gameplay, commentary, news, review, and meme videos that all revolve around Hitman. I occasionally also upload about other games, popular culture and vlogs!

The Host

Rieper1 is a 29 year old nerd and gamer from Australia. He started All Things Hitman as a hobby back in 2017 after he bought and played HITMAN on PS4. Slowly but surely, he eventually discoverd his niche within the Hitman Community by focusing on Hitman Lore. He gradually established a small but devoted following. Aside from Youtube and Hitman, he enjoys cooking, exploring outdoors, watching films, and spending time with his friends and family.
